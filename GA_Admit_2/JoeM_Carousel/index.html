Joe's Career

Joe has enjoyed a varied career in Silicon Valley. He has held corporate and strategic marketing, product marketing, finance, and operations management postions in both large and small companies. He co-founded Barrington Partners, a venture capital firm, and made series A investments in four companies that reached the public stock market. Currently he is Founder and Co-owner of Athentica P.B.C. a start-up that connects workforce support organizations to online, skills services.

Joe is married, has two children, and lives in Belmont, CA. He came to the Golden State from Boston, MA. Joe's passions include: all-things-California, computer programming, digital photography, cycling, hiking, and home improvement projects of all kinds.
